VegDog was created to help make travelling around both Ireland and Northern Ireland with our beloved dogs just a little easier. So are you ready for your next dog friendly adventure in Ireland?

My name is Sarah and this is Zeus (a four year old Alaskan Malamute) and Skadi (a two year old Husky-mix) and we created VegDog.

The idea behind Vegdog was partly made in memormy of the one and only Jinny. Our beautiful black lab that loved her adventures with her pack around Ireland, who saldy passed in 2020.

We always tried to include our dogs in our staycations and weekend hikes, but always struggled with finding everything we wanted, especially after us (humans in our pack) went plant based.

After using several apps and spending quite a while planning a recent adventure, VegDog was created to help make travelling around both Ireland and Northern Ireland with our beloved dogs just a little easier.

Instead of using multiple webpages and apps to organise your next dog friendly day out, why not use our deadicated wesbite maps and information.

So are you ready for your next dog friendly adventure in Ireland?
